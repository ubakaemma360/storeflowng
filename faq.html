Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay for Shopify myself?

No. We include and fully fund the first 12 months of Shopify in every package so you have zero platform cost at the beginning.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes. We require 50% deposit to start building. The remaining 50% is due when your store is ready for launch.

How long does it take to build my store?

Most stores are completed and launched within 7–14 working days after we receive your product information and 50% deposit.

Do you help with product photos and content?

Yes. We create professional photos, descriptions, and reels using your raw images or guide you on what to provide.

What if I want ongoing ads and social media management?

We offer monthly retainers after your store is live. Many clients start with the website and later add ads and content management.
